Order Matrix

STYLEPOT's Order Matrix will become the most important tool for your agents and other salespersons in their daily business.

The Order Matrix is integrated on all order-relevant pages, e.g. on the product pages and in the shopping cart. It allows to buy multiple color and size units with just a few clicks. In the shopping cart, more variants can be added without changing the page.

Practical, elegant, ingenious.

Agent Mode

Agent Mode was developed specifically for the needs of agents and is a tool for your sales. Your agents can control order transactions and interact digitally with customers, e.g. preparing shopping lists, shopping carts or creating entire orders.

The simple click-login principle allows to operate efficiently on the customer's account. Your customer can then view, adjust and finalize existing order proposals; a post-order function is also available.

Modern and efficient B2B tools are indispensable for your successful sales strategy.

Special functions

You have several customer-specific price lists and would like to manage them in your shop system? No matter if country lists, key account lists or other price lists, they can all be displayed in the STYLEPOT system and can be assigned to the respective customer. Furthermore, you have the option of creating protected content and having it displayed only to certain customers, such as special documents and pictures for download or appointment book.

Of course, your STYLEPOT system offers much more, be it sales reports, various statistics of topseller articles, or viewing invoices, various order histories, and more.
